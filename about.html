About Us

The Surface Skis team represents the cutting edge of skiing culture, combining raw talent, fearless progression, and an unmistakable sense of style. This crew of dedicated athletes thrives on innovation, constantly pushing the sport forward with daring tricks, smooth lines, and a fresh approach to both park and backcountry riding. Whether they’re dropping into deep powder, hitting urban rails, or perfecting new tricks in the park, the team is united by a shared passion for adventure and a relentless pursuit of the next big challenge. Each skier brings their unique flavor to the table, but together they embody the spirit of progression and creativity that defines Surface Skis. More than just riders, they’re a collective of innovators who continually inspire the next generation to rethink what’s possible on the mountain. With a blend of style, grit, and an unwavering dedication to the sport, the Surface Skis team doesn’t just conquer the slopes—they redefine them.
